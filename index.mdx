## Streamline Your Operations

Alternativa Verde is a comprehensive SaaS platform designed to manage the collection, tracking, and dispatch of used vegetable oil (AVU) across multiple collection centers. Built for efficiency and compliance, it helps your team maintain accurate records and streamline logistics.

## Key Features

- **Automatic ticket numbering** by state and year (e.g., AV-ARA-2026-0001)
- **Multi-center support** with independent operations and team management
- **Real-time dashboard** with charts and analytics
- **Excel export** for reports and compliance documentation
- **Material state tracking** (Filtrado, Bruto, Mezcla)
- **PostgreSQL database** for reliable data persistence
- **MINEC guide integration** for regulatory compliance
