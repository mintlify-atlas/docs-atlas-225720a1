## What is Alternativa Verde?

Alternativa Verde is an operational management system designed to help environmental collection centers track and manage material flows efficiently. The system provides complete control over material entries, exits, and movement tracking across collection centers.

## Who is it for?

Alternativa Verde is built for:

- **Collection centers** managing recyclable materials and waste
- **Environmental companies** coordinating multiple collection points
- **Operations staff** who need to track material generators, vehicles, and team members
- **Administrative teams** requiring visibility into material movements and dispatch history

### Core Features

- **Generator Registry**: Maintain a database of material generators (clients) with collection modes and contact information
- **Vehicle Management**: Track collection center vehicles and assign them to collections and dispatches
- **Material States**: Classify materials as Filtrado (Filtered), Bruto (Raw), or Mezcla (Mixed)
- **History & Reporting**: Review complete movement history for validation and corrections
- **Data Export**: Export operational data for reporting and analysis
